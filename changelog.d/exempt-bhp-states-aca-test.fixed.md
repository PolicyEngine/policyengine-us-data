Exempted NY (Essential Plan) and MN (MinnesotaCare) from the per-state ACA PTC calibration tests. Both states operate ACA §1331 Basic Health Programs that divert 138–200% FPL enrollees out of the Marketplace, so CMS APTC state targets are artificially low while the microsim (which does not model BHP) produces full-Marketplace PTC. This is a structural mismatch that can't be calibrated away. The target-side fix is tracked in #805; BHP upstream modeling is tracked separately.
